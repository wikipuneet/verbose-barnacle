FAQ & Aftercare

How do I prepare for my session? expand_more

Hydrate well, avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior, eat a solid meal, and wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being tattooed.

What is the healing timeline? expand_more

Surface healing usually takes 2-3 weeks, but the skin takes about 4-6 months to fully regenerate and for black/grey tones to settle.

Do you do cover-ups? expand_more

Yes, though cover-ups require an in-person consultation to assess the existing ink and discuss realistic design possibilities in realism style.

medical_services Quick Care Tips

  • check_circle Keep the bandage on for the time specified by HaTran.
