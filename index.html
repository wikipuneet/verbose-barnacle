HaTran — Master of the Needle

With over 15 years of experience, HaTran has built Century Ink into Emergent's premier destination for meaningful, high-quality custom tattoos. Specializing in a wide range of styles from intricate black and gray realism to vibrant color work, HaTran's approach combines technical precision with deep artistic intuition.

"Every tattoo tells a story — I help my clients turn their ideas into permanent art they can be proud of for a lifetime." — HaTran

HaTran's studio is a welcoming space where creativity thrives and clients feel heard. Whether it's your first tattoo or your twentieth, you'll leave Century Ink feeling empowered, cared for, and excited about your new ink.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long do appointments usually take? +
Small tattoos take 1-2 hours. Medium pieces 3-5 hours. Large custom work may require multiple sessions.
What is your minimum charge? +
$150 for small flash or minimal designs. We focus on quality over quantity.
Do you do walk-ins? +
We prefer appointments to ensure we give every client our full attention. Limited walk-in availability on slower days.
